    MarketGenie Scalping Recommendations Review

    MarketGenie scalping recommendations for active traders

    We recommend 6 stocks on a daily basis for scalpers that are expected to show the highest movement. The movement is computed at a 1-minute level, and the summation of profits across adjacent candles is used to determine the profit. The profit is thereafter normalized by their opening price to determine the highest value-for-money stocks at an intra-day level. We have been achieving an accuracy of over 70% in this recommendation, which is interpreted as, on average, 70% of our recommended stocks (roughly 4) end up in the top-20 most profitable stocks for the day from S&P 500 stocks.
